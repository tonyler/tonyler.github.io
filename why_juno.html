Why Juno?

The Cosmos ecosystem and Web3 are filled with exciting projects. I could dive into and spend my time in any of these well-designed, shiny gems backed by VCs, private investors, and professionals. However, my bet and personal challenge is Juno.

Why?

Juno is not a smart contract platform. It's a beautiful chaos challenging every decentralization supporter to make it work. Juno is a huge experiment. We either fail or succeed together.

Decentralized democracy is Juno's product, it will succeed and it will be beautiful
